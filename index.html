Privacy

Audio and transcripts stay on your Mac.

Listenara uses local Apple frameworks for transcription and translation. The browser preview runs on 127.0.0.1 with token-protected local controls.

Assistants can open and control the local preview. Transcript text is shared with an assistant only when you choose to ask that assistant to read, summarize, or rewrite it.

Assistant Integration

Built for Codex, Claude, and local-first agent workflows.

Listenara exposes a stable local CLI and preview contract. Assistants can launch the app, read status JSON, and open the local preview URL when their environment supports it.

The AI skill is adapter-based: one shared Listenara contract, with thin Codex and Claude adapters. The app remains the source of truth.
