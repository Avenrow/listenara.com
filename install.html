Install

Open Listenara on macOS.

Listenara v1 is a direct-download Mac app. It is ad-hoc signed and not Apple-notarized in v1, so macOS may show an extra warning the first time you open it.

  1. Download `Listenara-v1.0.0.dmg` from the Download page.
  2. Open the DMG and drag `Listenara.app` to Applications.
  3. If macOS blocks the first launch, Right-click `Listenara.app`, choose Open, then choose Open again.
  4. If needed, open System Settings, Privacy & Security, then approve opening Listenara.
  5. Grant microphone, speech recognition, and system-audio permissions only for audio you are authorized to record or transcribe.

Why this warning appears

Listenara is sold outside the App Store and v1 is released before joining the Apple Developer Program. The app still runs locally on your Mac; the warning is about Apple notarization status, not cloud processing.
