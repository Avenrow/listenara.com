Support

Support for Listenara users.

Email listenara.app@gmail.com for license, install, and product support.

Privacy Reminder

Keep license keys private. Do not send private audio, transcripts, saved sessions, or exports unless support specifically asks for the minimum diagnostic sample needed.

Activation Reset

A personal license can be activated on up to 3 personal Macs. If you replace, erase, or lose access to a Mac and hit the limit, email support from the purchase email address. Include the license ID if you have it. Do not send your full license key unless support specifically asks for it.

Install Help

Listenara v1 is distributed outside the App Store and is not Apple-notarized. If macOS blocks the first launch, follow the install instructions.
