Privacy

Local by default.

Listenara records, transcribes, translates, previews, stores, and exports locally on your Mac by default.

  • No cloud transcription service is required.
  • No account sign-in is required in v1.
  • No analytics are built into the app.
  • The browser preview runs on 127.0.0.1.
  • License activation is offline-verifiable inside the app.
  • Update checks only request latest.json from listenara.com.

AI assistants can control Listenara locally. Transcript text may enter an assistant chat only when you ask that assistant to read, summarize, rewrite, or otherwise process the text.

Update Checks

Listenara may check listenara.com for a small public version file and show a one-time prompt when a newer version is available. The update check does not send audio, transcripts, exports, license keys, or account data.

Payment Boundary

PayPal checkout and license email delivery happen outside the local app. Transcription, translation, saved sessions, and exports still stay local on your Mac by default.
